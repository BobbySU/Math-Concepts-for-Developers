Sales Potential Segmentation of Seasonal Customers 2026

This project segments seasonal customers—retail stores, bars, cafes, restaurants, and hotels—based on traffic, refrigeration capacity, equipment, urbanization, and historical sales. Using K-Means and hierarchical clustering, customers are grouped into four business-relevant categories: Gold, Silver, Bronze, and Iron.

Key Highlights:

Sales Potential Score combines traffic, refrigeration capacity, and historical sales to identify high-value outlets.

Gold & Silver segments represent top-performing customers for targeted sales and equipment allocation.

Bronze & Iron segments highlight growth opportunities or outlets needing support.

Clustering insights have been applied for real business decision-making, optimizing seasonal revenue and resource planning.

Future Work: Incorporate additional business variables, test alternative clustering methods, and use predictive modeling to enhance sales forecasts.
